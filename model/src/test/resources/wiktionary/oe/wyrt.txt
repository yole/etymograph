==Old English==

===Etymology 1===
From {{inh|ang|gmw-pro|*wurti}}, from {{inh|ang|gem-pro|*wurt-}}, the oblique stem of {{m|gem-pro|*wrōts}} (whence {{m|en|root}}).

Cognate with {{cog|osx|wurt}}, {{cog|goh|wurz}}, {{cog|non|urt}}, {{cog|got|𐍅𐌰𐌿𐍂𐍄𐍃}}.
